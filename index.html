About Me


I have:
  • B.Sc. in Computer Science Engineering from Yeditepe University, Istanbul, Turkey
  • M.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering from Sabanci University, Istanbul, Turkey
  • M.Sc. in Computer Science from University of Alabama, Tuscaloosa, AL

Currently, I am in Ph.D. program at Computer Science department of University of Alabama. My advisor is Dr. Jeff Gray. I am also working with Dr. Eugene Syriani and we are working on AToMPM Project as of now. Check what I am doing from this slide under 3 minutes.
